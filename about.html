About Aurangabad

Serving Across Aurangabad with Experience & Trust

Aurangabad is one of the fastest-growing cities in Maharashtra, known for its rich history, tourism, and industrial development. Famous places like Ajanta Caves, Ellora Caves, Bibi Ka Maqbara, and Daulatabad Fort attract visitors from all over India and abroad.

The city has a strong industrial presence with MIDC Waluj and Shendra areas, including automobile, pharma, and engineering companies. The culture of Aurangabad is a mix of tradition and modern lifestyle, making it a perfect place for living and business growth.

Weather in Aurangabad is mostly hot in summer and pleasant in winter, which makes proper packing and transport planning very important during shifting.

Harshit Moving Solutions has experience working with various sectors including private companies, government offices, banks, pharma companies, engineering firms, as well as professionals like army personnel, police officers, judges, lawyers, and civil engineers.

We also support social causes and NGOs by providing reliable transport and shifting services whenever required.

If you need any help related to house shifting, office relocation, or transport services in Aurangabad, feel free to contact us anytime. Our team is always ready to assist you with safe and affordable solutions.
